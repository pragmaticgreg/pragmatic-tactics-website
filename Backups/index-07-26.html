The Sales Black Hole

"Our sales team is reactive, not proactive. Leads slip through the cracks."
We aren't closing enough deals and lack the capacity to handle more. Leads arrive from everywhere, but nothing links them.

The Rhythm

Reduced correspondence to assess potential client fit. The team follows a consistent process and rhythm. Every lead is captured, nurtured, and visible in a living pipeline that shows exactly where tomorrow's revenue will come from.

Our Collaborative Approach:

  • We map every current lead-capture point, connect them to a right-sized CRM (sales pipeline)
  • Automate reminders, email templates, and set up a simple pipeline view
  • Team training sessions ensure the team log interactions without friction
2

The Task Tornado

"Tasks hide in email inboxes, sticky notes, and memory. Nothing ever feels 'done'."
Ownership is vague, priorities shift hourly, and progress stalls because the team is constantly firefighting.

The Hidden Cost

A consistent and transparent process that suits your team and work environment. Priorities are clear, details and expectations clearly conveyed. A step forward to get ahead of the firefighting.

Our Collaborative Approach:

  • We inventory recurring workflows and encompass them into defined processes
  • Collaboratively develop a process for managing tasks/responsibilities that fall outside the recurring processes
  • Coach the team on creating clear expectations, ownership, and sustainable accountability
3

The Onboarding Nightmare

"You need to increase team capacity but new hires take so long to get up to speed."
Training is ad-hoc, inconsistent, and pulls senior members away from their core responsibilities.

Onboarding Cost

New hires contribute meaningful work in their first week, feeling confident—not overwhelmed, other team members aren't left feeling burdened.

Our Collaborative Approach:

  • Systemization. Identify core processes within the business, document current model workflows
  • Apply easy efficiency wins through process or technology, develop quick reference guides
  • Develop checklists to support process consistency
4

Spreadsheet Sprawl

"Operating with duplicate data, conflicting formulas, and endless copy/paste."
Regular breakdowns and manual data entry make reporting slow, error-prone, and a major time sink.

Time Drain

Robust and sustainable spreadsheet system or graduation into dedicated operations software.

Our Collaborative Approach:

  • Identify the objective and detailed needs of the system, consolidate sheets into a single source of truth
  • Set proper permission and protected cells, identify and implement improvements
  • Ensure clear documentation and training documents are developed and automate imports/exports
5

Strategy Stall

"Strategic goals exist but lack tactical clarity and leadership consensus."
The team cycles through the same priority discussions without resolution. Resources feel stretched and timelines seem unrealistic.

Leadership Cost

A unified leadership team with crystal-clear tactical priorities that can realistically be executed with available resources. Strategic objectives translate into committed action with genuine consensus on trade-offs.

Our Collaborative Approach:

  • Pressure-test strategic goals against organizational capabilities
  • Facilitate structured discussions to achieve alignment on 3-5 mission-critical objectives
  • Map tactical roadmap for next 90 days and identify key obstacles
