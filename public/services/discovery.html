What Discovery Actually Gives You

Discovery is a low-commitment way to find out if we're the right fit. You walk away with a prioritized plan you can act on. I walk away understanding your business well enough to hit the ground running if we keep going.

It's a fixed-price engagement that gets us both up to speed. We map out how your business runs, identify where the real opportunities are, and build a prioritized plan around what's worth doing first.

The plan is yours. You can execute it with your internal team, take it to another provider, or keep building it out with me.

How It Works

1
Book & Intake

You sign up and complete a short intake form so I have the basic context of your business before we speak.

2
60-Minute Alignment Call

We meet to discuss your goals, where you feel the bottlenecks are, what you've already tried, and what workflows need the most attention.

3
Pre-Session Prep

With your permission, I introduce myself to key team members and gather initial information, like existing SOPs or documentation, to prep for our main session.

4
Half-Day Discovery Session

A deep dive (in-person or remote) where I work directly with you and key individuals to map out the specific workflows and identify operational gaps.

5
Analysis & Mapping

I compile the findings into visual workflow maps and interactive documentation, pinpointing the "low-hanging fruit" for optimization.

6
1-Hour Prioritization Call

We review the isolated projects, prioritize what to tackle first, and decide exactly who will execute them (your team, me, or a hybrid approach).
