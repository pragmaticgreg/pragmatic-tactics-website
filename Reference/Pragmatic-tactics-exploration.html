The gap between knowing and doing

"Work on your business, not in it" — "Delegate or drown" — "Systematize or struggle."

Solid advice. But here's the thing: knowing what to do and actually doing it are worlds apart.

That disparity is exactly why I started Pragmatic Tactics. After seeing brilliant businesses get stuck in this gap, I realized what was missing wasn't more advice—it was a partner focused on strategic execution, someone who works alongside you to make it happen.

How I Think About Your Business

Three lenses. One integrated approach.

People

Roles, accountability, communication patterns. Making sure the right people can do their best work.

Process

Workflows, systems, documentation. Turning tribal knowledge into repeatable operations.

Technology

Tools that actually fit. Digital solutions that work with your operations, not against them.
